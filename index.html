Privacy Policy

Elementum: Brick Blast Breaker — Last updated: March 23, 2026

Overview

Elementum: Brick Blast Breaker ("the App") is developed by EFCCIK Studio. We respect your privacy and are committed to being transparent about our data practices. The App does not require account creation or login.

Data We Collect

The App itself does not directly collect, store, or share any personal information. There are no user accounts, no login, and no personal data entry fields in the App.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services that may collect certain data:

Google AdMob — We display advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers, usage data, and ad interaction data to serve relevant ads. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

Firebase Analytics — We use Firebase Analytics to understand general app usage patterns such as which levels are played. This data is anonymous and aggregated. No personally identifiable information is collected. For more information, see Firebase Privacy Policy.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will take steps to remove it.

Data Retention

The App stores game progress (such as completed levels) locally on your device only. This data is not transmitted to any server. Uninstalling the App removes all local data.
